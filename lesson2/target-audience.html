W02 Assignment: Target Audience

Description

The website is designed for people who practice sports outdoors (jogging, biking, soccer and etc) to plan their activities according to the weather forecast.

Personas


Person 1: John Smith

Name: John Smith

Age: 23

Family status: Single

Interests: mountain climbing, biking, and reading

Likes to check the weather to plan his sports and everyday life


Person 2: Sara Jane

Name: Sara Jane

Age: 29

Family status: Married

Interests: Yoga, Watching movies, jogging

Likes to check the weather to plan her day if she is practicing sports in doors or outdoors.

Scenarios


Scenario 1:

Sarah wants to play beach volleyball with some friends, she can look up the weather forecast to program their game time and day when it's sunny.


Scenario 2:

Dennis and Kyle are planning to go waterfall hiking, they need to know that it won't rain the day of their hike.


Scenario 3:

Rose looks the forecast to know if she can ride her motorbike to work. Or if there is going to be bad weather she goes by car.


Scenario 4:

A farmer has to protect his crops from bad weather, especially really hot days or rain storms, he checks the forecast for any dangerous conditions for his farm.


Scenario 5:

John takes the bus and walks 15 minutes to get to work. He needs to check if it will rain then he has to take an umbrella.
